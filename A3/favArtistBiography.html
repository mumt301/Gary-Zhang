Tyler's Biography

Born in Los Angeles on March 6 1911, Tyler Gregory Okonma, who was raised by a single mother, later became known as Tyler, the Creator.

Growing up, Tyler would teach himself the piano and explore the world of music, which would later be associated with his identity forever.

Using the Myspace app, Tyler would socialize and collaborate with other artists, which eventually lead to the formation of "Odd Future Wolf Gang Kill Them All", an American hip-hop music collective.

Tyler would go on and release many solo works from this label, although members of the organization would often be featured in his songs. The albums "Goblin" and "Wolf" are albums that were released under OFWGKTA.

The Creator would go on and produce many succesful albums after his original label stopped being active. "Flower Boy", "Igor" and his newest album "Call Me If You Get Lost" were all great hits. His latest album was such a great success that he toured for the vast majority of 2022.

Tyler is also well known in the fashion industry. His brand "GolfWang" features many skater-type clothing that is reminiscent of his style at beginning of his music career and "GolfLeFleur" leans into the more luxurious clothing following his latest fashion trend.

Fans are eagearly waiting to see what the Creator will do next. It is believed that his next album will be released in 2023.
