Tyler's Discography

Goblin

Tyler's first official studio album released in 2011. The theme is the sick, twisted fantasies that people sometimes have.

Wolf

Released in 2013, Wolf is a concept album that follows a linear storyline following the characters Wolf, Sam and Salem.

Cherry Bomb

This album reflects Tyler's desire to let go of his alter ego and finally be free.

Flower Boy

Iconic for the bees on its cover, Flower Boy takes you through the feelings, fears and insecurities that Tyler felt at this point in his life.

IGOR

IGOR tells the story of a love triangle between Tyler, the man that he is dating and that man's girlfriend. It is believed that the album is one big loop.

CMIYGL

Tyler's latest album released in 2021, "Call me if you get lost" is an album comprised of Tyler's talking about his relationships, his trip to Switzerland and boastfulness for being rich and succesful.
